### Objective

- The goal is to be the **first player to discard all your cards** by playing valid moves based on shape or number.

---

### Card Composition

- The deck contains **54 cards** with five suits:
  - **Circles**
  - **Triangles**
  - **Crosses**
  - **Squares**
  - **Stars**
- Each suit has numbered cards (typically 1â€“14).
- There are **Whot cards** (wild cards) that allow players to change the current shape.

---

###  Game Setup

- **Players**: 2â€“6
- **Each player is dealt 6 cards**.
- Remaining cards form the **draw pile**.
- One card is flipped to start the **discard pile**.

---

### Game Logic

#### Valid Moves

- On your turn, play a card that matches either the **shape** or **number** of the top discard.
- If you can't play, **draw one card** from the draw pile.

####  Whot Card (Wild Card)

- Can be played on any card.
- When played, the player **declares a new shape**.
- The next player must play a card of the declared shape or another Whot.

---

###  Special Cards & Their Effects

| Card     | Effect         | Description                                 |
| -------- | -------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Whot** | Wild           | Can be played anytime. Declare a new shape. |
| **1**    | Hold On        | Player gets another turn.                   |
| **2**    | Pick Two       | Next player draws 2 cards and skips turn.   |
| **5**    | Pick Three     | Next player draws 3 cards and skips turn.   |
| **8**    | Suspension     | Next player skips their turn.               |
| **14**   | General Market | All players draw one card.                  |

> Note: These effects may vary slightly by house rules. You can allow players to toggle rule variants in your web version.

---

### Turn-Based Flow

1. **Player Turn**:

   - Play a valid card or draw from the pile.
   - Apply any special card effects.
   - End turn unless Hold On is played.

2. **Next Player**:

   - Respond to special card effects if applicable.
   - Play or draw.

3. **Repeat** until one player has no cards left.

---

### Win Conditions

- A player **wins the round** by discarding all their cards.

- Game can be played in rounds or to a point total.
